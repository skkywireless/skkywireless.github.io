"We are making a local presence in every major market and a global network of GSM mobile phones".

SKKY Wireless USA partners with manufacturers, operators, distributors, service centers, and retailers to turn inventory challenges into business opportunities. We specialize in the responsible and profitable redistribution of new and pre-owned phones, tablets, accessories, and other wireless equipment. We are making a local presence in every major market and a global network of GSM mobile phones. SKKY Wireless is a privately held company and located in Long Island, NY.

The purchasing and sales teams at SKKY Wireless have real-time insight into the pricing trends for devices being bought and sold all over the world. We help our partners understand the current and future value of their inventory, and offer them the best available price for products they are looking to buy or sell.
